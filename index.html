Article Title

AI tools are software applications and platforms that leverage artificial intelligence techniques—like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision—to automate tasks, analyze data, and make intelligent decisions.

Modern AI tools come in many forms, including cloud-based platforms like Google Cloud AI, Amazon SageMaker, and Microsoft Azure AI, as well as user-friendly applications like ChatGPT, Grammarly, and Canva’s AI features.
